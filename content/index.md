# Customs Law Knowledge Base

This wiki is a plain-English research index for customs-related law, official guidance, and source links.

It is intended to help readers find the primary legal source before they rely on a rule. It is not legal advice. Customs rules change often, especially tariff rates, quotas, sanctions, trade remedies, import taxes, restricted goods, and low-value shipment rules. Always check the official live database for the product, origin, destination, and date of import or export.

## How to Read These Pages

Use the overview sections to understand the legal area.

Use the official source links to confirm the current rule.

Treat live tariff, quota, sanctions, and trade-remedy databases as dynamic sources. A static wiki page can point to them, but it should not replace a fresh official lookup.

## Maintenance Rules

Each jurisdiction page should use short paragraphs, simple headings, and official links.

Avoid copying long legal text into the wiki. Summarize the issue in plain English and link to the official source.

Mark uncertain or fast-changing information clearly.

Prefer official sources, including customs authorities, legislation portals, tariff databases, tax authorities, official gazettes, WTO, WCO, OECD, UN, EU, ASEAN, GCC, Mercosur, AfCFTA, and other regional organization sources.
