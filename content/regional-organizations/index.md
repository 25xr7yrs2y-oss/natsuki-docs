# Regional Customs and Trade Organizations

This page maps regional organizations and economic unions that commonly affect customs law, tariff treatment, origin, and trade procedures.

Regional rules often interact with national customs law. Always check both the regional legal instrument and the implementing customs authority.

## European Union

Main page in this wiki: [EU Customs Legal Knowledge Base](../syntheses/eu-customs-legal-knowledge-base.md)

Official customs authority: [European Commission Taxation and Customs Union](https://taxation-customs.ec.europa.eu/customs_en)

Official legislation portal: [EUR-Lex](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/)

Official tariff database: [TARIC consultation](https://ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/dds2/taric/taric_consultation.jsp?Lang=en)

Key customs topics: Union Customs Code, Common Customs Tariff, Combined Nomenclature, TARIC, customs valuation, origin, AEO, special procedures, sanctions, and trade remedies.

## ASEAN

Official organization: [ASEAN](https://asean.org/)

Official trade agreement source: [ASEAN Trade in Goods Agreement](https://asean.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/ASEAN-Trade-in-Goods-Agreement.pdf)

Key customs topics: tariff liberalisation, ASEAN rules of origin, customs procedures, trade facilitation, non-tariff measures, and ASEAN Single Window cooperation.

Dynamic information note: Preferential origin and tariff treatment depend on the applicable ASEAN agreement, product-specific rule, member state implementation, and current tariff schedule.

## Gulf Cooperation Council

Official organization: [GCC Secretariat General](https://gcc-sg.org/)

Official customs source library: [GCC digital library customs materials](https://gcc-sg.org/en/MediaCenter/DigitalLibrary/Pages/default.aspx?FilterField4=Topic&FilterValue4=Customs)

Official common customs law PDF: [Common Customs Law of the GCC States](https://gcc-sg.org/en/MediaCenter/DigitalLibrary/Documents/1274258180.pdf)

Key customs topics: common customs law, common customs tariff, first point of entry, customs declarations, valuation, exemptions, transit, free zones, and customs offences.

Dynamic information note: GCC-wide rules must be checked with the customs authority of the member state handling the import or export.

## Mercosur

Official organization: [Mercosur](https://www.mercosur.int/en/)

Official tariff and nomenclature information: [Mercosur frequently asked questions](https://www.mercosur.int/en/about-mercosur/frequently-asked-questions)

Key customs topics: Mercosur Common Nomenclature, Common External Tariff, intra-Mercosur origin, exceptions, tariff databases, and trade remedy or safeguard coordination.

Dynamic information note: Common External Tariff rules and national exceptions can change. Use official Mercosur and national customs sources for current treatment.

## African Continental Free Trade Area

Official organization: [AfCFTA Secretariat](https://au-afcfta.org/)

Official African Union source example: [AfCFTA Rules of Origin Manual](https://au.int/sites/default/files/documents/37121-doc-AfCFTA_RULES_OF_ORIGIN_MANUAL.pdf)

Key customs topics: preferential tariff concessions, rules of origin, proof of origin, customs cooperation, trade facilitation, non-tariff barriers, and implementation by State Parties.

Dynamic information note: Preferential treatment depends on the State Parties, tariff concession schedules, origin rules, proof requirements, and national customs implementation.
